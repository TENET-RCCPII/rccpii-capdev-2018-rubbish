What is the RCCP II Capacity Development Initiative?

The Rural Campus Connectivity Project II is a follow-on project from RCCP I which ran from 2010 - 2014. The project is funded by the South African Department of Higher Education and Training, managed by Universities of South Africa (USAF), and implemented by the Tertiary Education and Research Network (TENET) of South Africa. Although RCCP I focused mostly on connecting the rural campuses to the network, RCCP II have a significant capacity development component to assist tertiary education facilities with adoption of internet for research and teaching and learning.This website will host information about capacity development opportunities that exist for staff and students at South African tertiary education facilities through the RCCP II project in 2018.

Connect

Find out how your university is involved with RCCP II or other initiatives to get you connected to the Internet.

Communicate

Learn how to connect virtually with researchers, lecturers, under and postgraduate students and more through video conferencing facilities running on a laptop, mobile phone, lab computer, or tablet. You can get access to mentoring, training, and communities of practice by connecting online.

Collaborate

Participate in workshops (online or in person) and learn about tools that can be used for online collaboration in your communities of practice. Then try these tools out in your own collaborations.

Convert

There are loadsd of materials available online that can be re-used, modified, and converted to suit your needs - in teaching and learning, as well as research. Learn about open licenses, online repositories for data, slides, and more, and see how you can prevent reinventing the wheel in your work by re-using materials on the web.
